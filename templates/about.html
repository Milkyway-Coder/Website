About SoulMate

Bringing hearts together since 2020

Our Story

SoulMate was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to help people find meaningful, lasting relationships based on compatibility, shared values, and genuine connection.

Our founders, Amruth and Manojna, experienced firsthand the challenges of finding a compatible life partner through traditional means. After his own journey led him to his wife through a matrimonial service, he realized there was an opportunity to create something better - a platform that focuses not just on social and family criteria, but on true compatibility.

Since our launch in 2020, we've helped thousands of couples find their perfect match, leading to hundreds of successful marriages and countless love stories.

Our Mission

To help individuals find their perfect life partner through a thoughtful, values-based matching approach.

Authentic Connections

We believe in fostering genuine relationships based on compatibility and shared values, not just surface-level attributes.

Safety & Privacy

We prioritize the security of our users with strict verification processes and privacy controls.

Inclusivity

We welcome people from all backgrounds, cultures, and communities, creating a diverse and inclusive platform.

Our Approach

At SoulMate, we take a different approach to matchmaking:

  • Compatibility-Focused: Our algorithm goes beyond basic demographics to match people based on personality, values, and life goals.
  • Quality Over Quantity: We believe in presenting fewer, more compatible matches rather than overwhelming users with options.
  • Verified Profiles: Every profile undergoes a verification process to ensure authenticity and build trust.
  • User Privacy: We give users control over who can view their profile and how much information they share.
  • Continuous Improvement: We regularly update our matching algorithm based on successful matches and user feedback.
